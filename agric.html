Hawaz Agricultural Services Ltd

RC No. 8290819

Hawaz Agricultural Services Ltd is a fully incorporated agribusiness enterprise under the Company and Allied Matters Act (CAMA), 2020, committed to driving growth and sustainability in the agricultural sector. We operate as a diversified agricultural company with a strong presence in mechanised crop farming, animal husbandry, agro-processing, and international exports.

Our farming operations cover the cultivation of key food and cash crops such as oil palm, yam, cassava, maize, beans, soya beans, and guinea corn. Alongside crop production, we run structured livestock farms focused on cattle, sheep, goats, and poultry, ensuring a steady supply of both meat and dairy products.

Beyond local farming, we extend our reach to the global marketplace through the export of premium Nigerian produce, including palm oil, cassava and its derivatives, hibiscus flowers, and soya beans. This export arm not only strengthens Nigeria’s agricultural footprint abroad but also creates opportunities for local farmers and communities.

With a strong commitment to innovation, food security, and sustainable practices, we leverage modern technology and mechanisation to deliver high-quality agricultural products, empower local farmers, create jobs, and contribute meaningfully to the growth of Nigeria’s economy.

We are also engaged in the procurement and distribution of fertilizers and insecticides that will help to bringing about high yields.
