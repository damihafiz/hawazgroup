Hawaz Oil & Gas Services Ltd

RC No. 8288954

Our company was incorporated under the Company and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) on 21st February, 2025, with a mandate to provide innovative, reliable, and sustainable solutions across the oil, gas, and petroleum products sector. As a dynamic and forward-looking enterprise, we are positioned to meet the growing energy demands of Nigeria and the West African sub-region through high-quality products, efficient services, and customer-focused operations.

We operate as independent marketers of petroleum products, ensuring the availability, distribution, and retail of a wide variety of essential fuels and lubricants. Our portfolio covers:

Petrol (PMS)
Gasoline
Engine Oil and Lubricants
Kerosene (DKP)
Liquefied Petroluem Gas (Cooking Gas)
Hydraulic Fluids
Gearbox and Axle Oil
Other specialized petroleum derivatives

To support accessibility and efficiency, we are also engaged in filling station development and operations, providing end-users with convenient access to quality fuel and petroleum services.
Beyond retail and distribution, our operations extend into comprehensive oil and gas services, spanning the upstream, downstream, and industrial gas segments. This includes:

  • Stocking, storage, and bulk distribution of petroleum and industrial gas products
  • Refining, re-branding, and conversion of crude oil and derivatives into market-ready products
  • Exploration and development of oil resources
  • Marketing and sales of agro-allied petroleum-related products

Our vision is to establish a strong commercial presence across West Africa, leveraging local sales representatives, robust logistics, and advanced infrastructure to deliver competitive, customer-focused energy solutions.

With a commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation, we aim to be a trusted partner in the energy value chain, powering industries, communities, and households with efficiency and reliability.
