Hawaz 774 Estates and Diasporan Villa Nig. Ltd

RC No. 8289106

The Hawaz 774 Estates and Diasporan Villa Nig. Ltd was duly incorporated under the Company and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020 on 21st February, 2025. The Company is legally authorised to operate across various segments of the real estate and property sector, including Property Development, Project Development, Investment, Property Consultancy, Land Agency (Sales and Lettings), Estate Development, Facility Management, Contract Facilitation, Mass Housing Development, and Real Estate Brokerage.

Recognising the housing deficit in Nigeria—currently estimated at over 28 million units—the Company is strategically positioned to contribute to bridging this gap. By forging partnerships with reputable international real estate developers, we aim to deliver innovative housing solutions that align with the Federal Government’s initiatives to provide affordable and sustainable housing for Nigerians.

Are you a Diasporan? Our company is poised to meet your housing needs in Nigeria. We can help you realize your dreams of owning houses in any part of Nigeria. Through us, we can help you address the problems of family members defrauding you of your hard earnings under the pretexts of building houses for you, with a huge disappointment that characterizes family members getting involved on your behalf.

As a registered member of the Real Estate Developers Association of Nigeria (REDAN), the Company is actively collaborating with a Federal Government agency to pioneer estate developments in Nigeria, with a unique focus on utilising bamboo as a sustainable and cost-effective building material. This innovative approach underscores our commitment to delivering value, promoting eco-friendly construction, and supporting national housing objectives.
