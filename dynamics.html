Hawaz International Dynamics Ltd

Registered with RC No. 8338045, our company was incorporated on 13th March 2025 under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020 to provide a broad spectrum of services within the aviation eco-system.

Our core mandate includes acting as a trusted agent to foreign airlines seeking to establish operations on Nigerian routes. We assist in obtaining the required operational permits and regulatory approvals from the relevant aviation authorities, ensuring a seamless entry into the Nigerian market.

As a licensed holder of the Air Travel Organiser’s Licence (ATOL), we are authorized to deliver end-to-end travel solutions, which covers:

International travel arrangements for tourism, business, and religious pilgrimage.
Airline ticketing and reservations with global carriers.
Visa procurement services to simplify international travel.
Comprehensive travel agency support for individuals, groups, and organizations.

In addition, we offer specialized consultancy services across all aspects of Civil Aviation, providing expert guidance on compliance, operations, safety standards, and strategic growth.

Driven by innovation, our vision is to revolutionize Nigeria’s aviation landscape by deploying cutting-edge technologies that unlock new opportunities, improve efficiency, and tap into the vast potential that has long remained underutilized in the industry.
