Hawaz and Company's Ltd

At Hawaz and Company’s Ltd, we understand that business is not just about products and services—it’s about trust, timing, and tailored solutions. Founded with a commitment to excellence, we operate as a multi-purpose company providing dependable services across logistics, distribution, and general supply.

We currently serve clients across the North Central region of Nigeria and beyond, delivering value through reliable operations and a diverse portfolio. From logistics and cargo movement to the distribution of essential goods, we are driven by innovation, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
